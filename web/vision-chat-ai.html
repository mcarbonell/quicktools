What is AI Vision Chat?

AI Vision Chat uses Google Gemini Vision to analyze images and answer questions about them. Ask about objects, text, colors, composition, or get detailed descriptions of any image.

How to chat with images?

  1. Upload an image you want to analyze
  2. Ask a question or request analysis
  3. Get detailed AI responses about the image
  4. Ask follow-up questions for more details

Use cases for AI vision chat

  • Identify objects and scenes in photos
  • Extract and read text from images (OCR)
  • Get accessibility descriptions for images
  • Analyze product photos and designs
  • Understand diagrams and charts
