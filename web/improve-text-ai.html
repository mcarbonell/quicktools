What is AI Text Improvement?

AI Text Improvement uses Chrome's Rewriter API or Google Gemini to enhance your writing. Fix grammar, improve clarity, adjust tone, and make text more professional or concise automatically.

How to improve text with AI?

  1. Paste your text that needs improvement
  2. Select improvement mode: Grammar, Clarity, Professional, Casual, or Concise
  3. Click 'Improve Text' to enhance writing
  4. Copy or download the improved version

Use cases for text improvement

  • Fix grammar and spelling errors
  • Make emails more professional
  • Improve clarity of technical writing
  • Adjust tone for different audiences
  • Make text more concise and readable
