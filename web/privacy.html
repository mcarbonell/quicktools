Privacy Policy & Cookies

100% Local Processing

FastTools is designed to process all data in your browser. Files you upload never leave your device:

  • Images are processed using Canvas API
  • PDFs are read with PDF.js in the browser
  • Text and JSON are manipulated locally
  • No backend or cloud storage

Cookies and Local Storage

This site uses minimal cookies and local storage:

  • Essential cookies: none
  • Analytics (optional): Google Analytics / Plausible
  • Advertising: Google AdSense (when active)
  • LocalStorage: User preferences (theme, last format used, etc.)

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display relevant ads. This involves:

  • Third-party cookies from Google
  • Interest-based personalization
  • We do not share personal data with advertisers

You can manage your advertising preferences at Google Ads Settings.

Your Rights

  • Access and control over your data
  • Option to reject non-essential cookies
  • Data portability (although we don't store anything)
