What is OCR (Optical Character Recognition)?

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) uses AI vision to extract text from images. Our tool uses Chrome's local AI (Gemini Nano) to perform OCR 100% offline with complete privacy. Convert images to text, Markdown, or structured JSON.

How to extract text from images?

  1. Upload an image containing text (JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP)
  2. Select output format: Plain Text, Markdown, or JSON
  3. Click 'Extract Text' to perform OCR with local AI
  4. Copy or download the extracted text

Use cases for OCR

  • Digitize printed documents and books
  • Extract text from screenshots and photos
  • Convert business cards to contact data
  • Transcribe handwritten notes (with AI assistance)
  • Extract data from receipts and invoices
