What is PDF splitting and why is it useful?

PDF splitting is the process of extracting specific pages or page ranges from a large PDF document to create smaller, focused PDF files. This technique uses precise page extraction algorithms to maintain document quality, formatting, and content integrity. Splitting PDFs helps with document organization, sharing specific sections, and creating targeted documents for different purposes. Learn more about PDF page extraction and document management strategies.

How to use the PDF Splitter tool?

  1. Upload your PDF by dragging it to the drop area or clicking to select
  2. Enter the page numbers you want to extract (e.g., 1, 3-5, 8)
  3. Click 'Split PDF' to start the extraction process
  4. Download the new PDF with the selected pages

Common use cases for PDF splitting

  • Extract specific chapters or sections from large books or reports
  • Share individual pages or forms without the entire document
  • Create presentation slides from multi-page PDF documents
  • Separate invoice pages or receipts for different clients
  • Archive specific documents like certificates or legal pages
