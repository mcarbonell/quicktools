What is AI Audio Transcription?

AI Audio Transcription uses Chrome's local AI (Gemini Nano) to convert speech to text completely offline. Transcribe audio files or record live audio with 100% privacy - no data sent to servers.

How to transcribe audio with AI?

  1. Upload an audio file (MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, WebM)
  2. Or click 'Record Audio' to capture live speech
  3. Click 'Transcribe Audio' to convert to text
  4. Copy or download the transcription result

Use cases for audio transcription

  • Transcribe meeting recordings and interviews
  • Convert voice notes to searchable text
  • Create subtitles for videos and podcasts
  • Generate meeting minutes from audio
  • Transcribe lectures and educational content
